Data Processing Policy

At Data Mirroring, we prioritize the privacy and security of your data. This page outlines how we process your data when you use our service hosted on Heroku.

Coding for Privacy

This app is designed to keep your data secure. When you upload a file, it’s stored in a temporary location that only you can access through your session. This means that no one else can download or view your files. Once you download your processed data, the file is automatically deleted to keep your information safe. The app also uses secure connections (HTTPS) to protect your data while you're using it, and it only allows specific, safe file types to be uploaded. In short, your data is handled carefully, and it's deleted after you’ve downloaded it, ensuring your privacy throughout.

Heroku's Data Storage Policy

Our service is hosted on Heroku, a platform that emphasizes data security and privacy. Heroku provides a reliable and scalable cloud platform that complies with industry standards for security. They ensure that your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, preventing unauthorized access. For more details about Heroku's policies, you can visit their official documentation on privacy and compliance.

GDPR Compliance

We are committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Heroku also adheres to GDPR guidelines, ensuring that your data is managed responsibly and ethically. For more information on how Heroku handles data under GDPR, please refer to their GDPR Compliance page.

Key Takeaways

  • No data is stored by us after processing.
  • Heroku follows strict data privacy policies.
  • We comply with GDPR regulations to protect your rights.

If you have any questions regarding our data processing policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.
