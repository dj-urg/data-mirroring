Data Processing & Security Policy

Data Mirroring is build to prioritize the privacy and security of your data. This page outlines the security measures we have implemented to protect your information when using our application hosted on Heroku.

Coding for Security

Our application is built with a privacy-first approach. When you upload a file, it is temporarily stored in a secure location and only accessible during your session. Once you terminate your session, close your browser, your data is automatically deleted, ensuring no persistent storage of personal data. We take steps to clean up temporary files both during and after your session to protect your privacy.

Our design is fully open-source, please see: Data Mirroring Github Page.

Hosting & Infrastructure Security

Our application is hosted on Heroku's EU-based servers, ensuring compliance with European data protection regulations. Heroku encrypts all data in transit and at rest, preventing unauthorized access.

For more details, please visit: Heroku's Security Policy.

In addition, we use HTTPS encryption to secure your data in transit, and we strictly allow only specific, safe file types to minimize security risks. To enhance security, we have implemented Expedited WAF (Web Application Firewall) to protect our application.

For more details, please visit: Heroku's Expedited WAF Add-on.

GDPR Compliance & Data Privacy

We rely on highly trusted and secure third-parties to be fully GDPR compliant and ensure:

  • All data is processed in the EU, preventing international data transfers.
  • No persistent storage of personal data.

For more details, refer to: Heroku GDPR Compliance.

Key Takeaways

  • Our application is designed with a privacy-first approach.
  • We use Heroku EU-based servers for compliance with GDPR.
  • Expedited WAF protects against cyber threats.

If you have any concerns or questions regarding our data processing and security policies, please contact us at daniel.jurg@vub.be.
