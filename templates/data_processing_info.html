Data Processing Policy

At Data Mirroring, we prioritize the privacy and security of your data. This page outlines how we process your data when you use our service hosted on Heroku.

We do not retain any of your personal data processed via this web service. All data provided for processing is handled in real-time and is discarded immediately after processing. This ensures that your data remains private and secure at all times.

Heroku's Data Storage Policy

Our service is hosted on Heroku, a platform that emphasizes data security and privacy. For more details about Heroku's policies, you can visit their official documentation on privacy and compliance.

GDPR Compliance

We are committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Heroku also adheres to GDPR guidelines, ensuring that your data is managed responsibly and ethically. For more information on how Heroku handles data under GDPR, please refer to their GDPR Compliance page.

Key Takeaways

  • No data is stored by us after processing.
  • Heroku follows strict data privacy policies.
  • We comply with GDPR regulations to protect your rights.
