Netflix Dashboard

We'd love to help you gain insights from your Netflix viewing data! Please upload your downloaded Netflix CSV files using the form below. You can include files such as: IndicatedPreferences.csv, MyList.csv, Ratings.csv, SearchHistory.csv, or ViewingActivity.csv. If you are not sure where to find these files, please refer to our GitHub Wiki for instructions on how to upload your data.

Once you have your files ready, simply click the Choose File button to select your files (you can select multiple files at once or one after the other), then press the Add to List button to add them for analysis. To begin the analysis, press the Analyze Data button. Once your data is processed, you'll see visualizations and have the option to download your data in CSV or Excel format.
